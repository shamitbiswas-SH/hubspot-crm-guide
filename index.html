How to Associate a Deal with the Company #

Ensure your deal is properly associated with the company. If not linked, HubSpot will be unable to track data related to the deal and account activities.

  1. Open the Deal record
  2. Click "Add" in the Companies panel on the right-hand side
  3. Enter the accurate account name in the search bar
  4. Select the appropriate account from the results
⚠ Important Always verify the company association before saving. An unlinked deal cannot be properly tracked or reported in HubSpot.

How to Log Activities #

Logging a Task or Demo

  1. Open the corresponding Deal, Account, or Contact
  2. Click on the Tasks tab
  3. Add subject line, notes, and task owner
  4. Assign the task to a team member by clicking "Assigned to"

Logging a Meeting

  1. Select "Log Meeting" to record an existing meeting
  2. To create a new meeting, ensure Outlook or Gmail is connected to HubSpot first
  3. Click "Log Meeting" and fill in attendees, outcome, type, date, and time
  4. Associate the contacts you connected with during the meeting
Note To set up meeting creation, go to Settings → Calendar → Connect Your Calendar.

Connecting Your Calendar

  1. Click "Create a Meeting" to reach the calendar setup page
  2. Select "Connect Your Calendar"
  3. Choose your provider — Google/Gmail, Microsoft Outlook, or Exchange
  4. Click "Connect Your Calendar" and sign in
  5. You are now ready to create meetings directly from HubSpot
  6. Use "Attendees" when creating a meeting to add deal or account contacts
Tip Sending meeting invitations from HubSpot tracks all meetings automatically in both HubSpot and your calendar.

How to Add a Meeting Link in Email #

  1. Click "Email" then "Create an email"
  2. Click "Meeting" — two sub-options appear:
    • Insert Proposed Time — specify slots; recipients click to confirm.
    • Insert Scheduling Link — recipients access your calendar and pick their time.
  3. "Manage Meeting Link" — set availability, configure confirmation and reminder emails
  4. Edit the link text, e.g., "Meet with [Your Name]"

Insert Proposed Time

After clicking Insert Proposed Time, a calendar opens. Select available slots and click Insert Times. Slots appear inline in the email — recipients click any slot to confirm.

Scheduling Page Settings

  • Scheduling page URL — your custom availability link
  • Duration options — 15 min, 30 min, 60 min
  • Time zone — set automatically from HubSpot settings
  • Availability window — e.g., Mon–Fri 9:00 AM–5:00 PM
  • Confirmation email — sent automatically upon booking
  • Pre-meeting reminder — configurable (default: 1 hour before)

How to Log and Make Calls #

Logging a Call

  • Navigate to the Contact, Company, or Deal record
  • Click the "Calls" tab then "Log a Call"
  • Use the Call Outcome dropdown to categorise the call
  • Fill in call description, date, and time
  • Click "Log Activity" to save

Activating the Calling Function

  1. Click the "Calling" tab within a Contact or Deal record
  2. If not yet set up, click "Make a Phone Call"
  3. Click "Set Up My Own Number"
  4. Enter your phone number and extension (if applicable)
  5. Choose verification method — SMS or phone call
  6. Click "Text me" or "Call me" to receive your verification code
  7. Enter the verification code to complete setup

Method 1 — Outlook Desktop Add-in (Windows Only)

  1. Download and install the HubSpot Outlook Desktop Add-in from the HubSpot marketplace
  2. Run the setup installer
  3. Restart Outlook — it will prompt for your HubSpot credentials
  4. "HubSpot Sales" will appear in your inbox ribbon once installed
  5. View your activity feed by clicking the HubSpot sprocket in your Windows system tray
  6. The sprocket changes from gray to orange when active — ensure it is enabled
  7. Emails sent with HubSpot tracking will auto-BCC to your portal (6655148@bcc.hubspot.com)
Note Outlook for Mac is NOT supported by the Desktop Add-in. Mac users should use Method 2.

Method 2 — Office 365 Add-in

  1. Go to Microsoft AppSource and search for "HubSpot Sales"
  2. Click "Get It Now"
  3. Enter your official email address and click Sign in
  4. After confirming your email, click "Get It Now" again to complete installation
⚠ Important Do NOT install both add-ins on the same device — they will conflict and cause issues.

Connecting Office 365 to HubSpot CRM

  1. Click Settings (cog icon, top right)
  2. Click General → Email Integrations
  3. "Connect an inbox" → Connect personal email
  4. Select Office 365 and enable it
  5. Follow the instructions and choose the appropriate inbox

Troubleshooting — Emails Not Tracked

  1. Ensure HubSpot Sales for Windows is installed
  2. If tray icon is grayed out, right-click sprocket → Enable Notifications
  3. Before sending, ensure the "Track email" checkbox is selected
  4. If still not tracked, reinstall the HubSpot Outlook Add-in

Call Recording Sync with HubSpot #

Making and Recording a Call

  • Click "Make a Phone Call" on a contact or deal record
  • Click the "Record" button to begin recording
  • Accept the consent pop-up — always inform the other party before recording
  • Take notes during the call in the Notes panel
  • After the call, select the outcome and call type, then click Save Call
  • Review call details and recordings in the Activity or Calls tab
⚠ Important Some countries and states require two-party consent to record calls. Always inform the other party before recording.

Breeze Prospecting Agent ★ NEW #

The Breeze Prospecting Agent is a 24/7 AI-powered BDR that understands your business, monitors prospects for buying signals, researches target accounts, personalises outreach, and engages prospects to help sales teams build pipeline faster.

What the Prospecting Agent Does

  • Automated account research: Pulls from HubSpot data plus external sources (websites, blog posts, news)
  • Selling profiles: Customises outreach for different products, personas, and target markets
  • Buying signals: Identifies job postings, funding rounds, leadership changes, and tech stack changes
  • Buyer committee insights: Maps every decision-maker in the deal using AI
  • Personalised email drafts: Full customer context from past CRM interactions
  • Full prospecting lifecycle: From signal detection to email drafting — all in one place

Where to Access the Prospecting Agent

  • Sales Workspace — main dashboard
  • Prospecting Agent app — dedicated interface
  • Target Accounts app — new in 2025
Info 28-day free trial available at $1 per recommended lead (pay-per-result). Early users report 2× industry benchmark outreach response rates.

Setting Up Selling Profiles

  1. Navigate to Settings → Breeze → Prospecting Agent
  2. Click "Create Selling Profile"
  3. Define target product, ideal customer persona, and target market
  4. Add your company's value proposition and differentiators
  5. Save and assign the profile to reps or teams

Using the Prospecting Agent — Step by Step

  1. Go to Sales Workspace or Prospecting Agent app
  2. Select target accounts — agent automatically begins research
  3. Review buyer committee insights and signal alerts
  4. Review AI-drafted personalised emails for each prospect
  5. Edit and send — or enable auto-engagement

Sales Workspace & Smart Deal Progression ★ NEW #

Sales Workspace

The Sales Workspace is an all-in-one home where sales reps gather context, prioritise prospects, and take action in a unified interface. It replaced the previous Prospecting Workspace and was fully deployed in January 2025.

  • Pipeline management: View and manage all deals with "Next Meeting" and "Last Activity" columns
  • Prospecting: Prioritise target accounts with guided AI-generated next actions
  • AI research summaries: AI-generated company research on deal pages — no manual research needed
  • Guided actions: Automatically generated suggestions based on deal stage and buyer signals

Smart Deal Progression (New 2025/2026)

Smart Deal Progression activates after every sales call, analysing the transcript alongside full deal history to provide:

  • CRM field update suggestions: Based on what was discussed in the call
  • Follow-up email draft: Tailored to the conversation context
  • Action items & next steps: Aligned with pipeline and deal stage definitions
  • Forecasting alignment: Suggestions based on your pipeline definitions and logic
Tip Use Smart Deal Progression after every prospect call to keep CRM records current without manual data entry — ensuring accurate pipeline reporting and forecasting.

Customer Success Workspace (New 2025)

Dedicated workspace for Customer Success Managers featuring:

  • Customer health scores (0–100 scale) with configurable factors
  • Schedule, tasks, and books of business in one view
  • Real-time alerts on customer health score changes
  • Multi-team support for large-scale CS organisations

Breeze Intelligence & Data Enrichment ★ NEW #

Breeze Intelligence is HubSpot's data enrichment and buyer intent layer, pulling from 200M+ buyer and company profiles.

Key Capabilities

  • Data Enrichment: Automatically enriches Company and Contact records with verified data from 200M+ profiles
  • Buyer Intent Scoring: Surfaces companies showing purchase interest based on website visits and engagement
  • Form Shortening: Pre-fills web forms to increase conversion rates
  • Lead Scoring: AI-powered scoring to identify high-engagement, high-fit prospects
Info Breeze Intelligence credits start at $42/month for 100 credits. Pay-as-you-go available.

Accessing Breeze Intelligence

  1. Navigate to any Contact or Company record in HubSpot
  2. Click "Enrich" or enable auto-enrichment in Settings → Breeze Intelligence
  3. For Buyer Intent, go to Sales → Target Accounts and filter by intent signals
  4. Monitor credit usage in Settings → Breeze Intelligence

Using Buyer Intent in Your Workflow

  1. Go to Marketing → Breeze Intelligence → Buyer Intent
  2. Define intent criteria — select pages to track (pricing, demo, product pages)
  3. Review accounts flagged as showing intent
  4. Route high-intent accounts to the Prospecting Agent for personalised outreach
  5. Set up workflow automations to notify reps when accounts hit intent thresholds
